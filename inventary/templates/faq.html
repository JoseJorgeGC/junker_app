In the entry section, you can add a car by filling in all the required fields with its information. When uploading the car title, it must be in PDF format, and for the image, it can be either JPEG or PNG.

You can sell a car in different ways. In the inventory section, you can choose the car you want to sell and follow the provided steps. You can also use the "Cars pending for scrapping" tab to complete the sale.

To junk a car, the first thing you should do is mark the car you want to junk as pending in the "Cars" tab within the table displaying all the cars in the inventory. Click on the bookmark icon in the "Actions" column of the table. After that, go to the "Pending for Junking" tab and in the table displaying all the pending cars, you can press the X icon on the car you want to junk and follow the steps requested to junk the car.
